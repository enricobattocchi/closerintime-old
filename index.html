Instructions

Choose two events by typing in the text fields to get suggestions. You can also search by year or by category:

  • music
  • history
  • computer
  • art
  • film
  • building
  • science
  • book
  • sport
  • pop culture
  • personal
  • submitted

After you've chosen two events, the timeline will be updated to show the timespans.

You can add your personal events (e.g. the day of your birth) that will be stored locally in your browser and not shared with anyone.

If you add an event as a suggestion, it will be submitted to be included in the global database of events.

The dates chosen for some events may be approximate when a precise dating is not possible. Supporting one dating hypothesis against others is beyond the scope of this app. We suggest to read the Wikipedia article (using the button) to learn more about each event, person or object.
